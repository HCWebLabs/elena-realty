The Area

Ashford Valley

Where architecture meets landscape.

Ashford Valley has quietly become one of the most sought-after addresses for those who value architectural integrity and natural beauty. Originally developed in the 1950s and 60s by a consortium of progressive builders, the area attracted architects who wanted to experiment with modern residential design.

Today, you'll find an extraordinary concentration of mid-century originals alongside thoughtfully designed contemporary homes—all set against rolling hills, mature oak groves, and some of the most striking vistas in the region.

About
